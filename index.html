Consumer Awareness

Part of our mission is protecting the integrity of the marketing industry. Be wary of:

  • "You've Won!" Scams: Legitimate companies won't ask for fees to claim prizes.
  • Fake Giveaways: Always verify the social media profile's blue checkmark.
  • Phishing: Never click links in unsolicited "prize" texts or emails.
